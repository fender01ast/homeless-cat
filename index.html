Classic sad story of our cattos

Our cats' life story is quite a touching one, they were adopted but then abandoned by the most stone-hearted creature - human. But there were lucky enough because all our 777 cats have now found shelter in an abandoned fish factory. Irrespective of their sad unfair story, we still believe there are people who care out there - the right ones. And certainly every furry must have the right human, so we are ready to place our coats in safe hands.
